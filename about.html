What's this all about?

Ever been in a virtual meeting where you don't think you are needed, but you just have to be a part of it anyway? What if you don't have to?

This intelligent tool will listen for the keywords you add to the listener and notify you on your phone when that keyword is heard.

Known Issues: It works only on Google Chrome.
